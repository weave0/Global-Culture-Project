- Avoid direct eye contact with authority

- Establish trust slowly  
- Avoid rushed business talk  

**Workplace Insights:**  
- Community decisions may override individuals  
- Ritual involvement may impact scheduling

- Rooted in ancestral land ties and yam cult ceremonies
